How to Use
1. Choose a circuit tab above.
2. Click Wire Mode, then click a pin to start a wire.
3. Click a second pin to complete it.
4. Scroll wheel = zoom in/out.
5. Right-click & drag = pan canvas.
6. In Select mode, drag components.
7. Double-click a wire to delete it.
8. ESC cancels current wire.
9. Click Grade when finished.
